# User Story: Have Audio Recording accissble in app

As a logged in user, who already did several voice recordings,
I want the Audio file and audio recording to be accessible from within the app, such that I can play it back in a convenient and quick manner, and hear what I said. This also helps me to convince myself the audio transkript is accurate.

## Additional Information

- See image `image-audio-player.png` for where the audio player shall be located on the page.

## Acceptance Criteria


- The already recorded audio can be played back with a basic and easy to use audio player on the recording session.
- The audio player must allow to 
  - Start/Stop playback
  - Adjust the Volume level
  - Seek via a timeline through the entire recording
- When clicking on a word in the Transcript, the audio player automatically advances to the corresponding time code in the recording, such that I can hear this word right when I press play. So playback starts right from the selected word in the text.
- When advanding the timeline in the audio player (dragging the seekhead in the timeline), the corresponding part of the text is highlighted.
